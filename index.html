I take on focused engagements — not open-ended staff augmentation. Each project has a defined scope, a measurable outcome, and a built-in exit. Here's what that looks like in practice.

01
Clear scope & outcome
We agree upfront on what success looks like — a delivered system, a migrated platform, a solved integration problem. No open-ended mandates.
